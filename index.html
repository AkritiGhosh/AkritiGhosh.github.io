Akriti Ghosh is a Software Engineer currently living & working in Bhilai, India. As a CS major, she gets a thrill out of challenging, fast-paced, diverse & complex problems.

While Browsing through different areas of focus like web development, mobile development, IoT, cybersecurity etc, she found her purpose in the area that connects her desirable fields - mathematics, programming, algorithms and artificial intelligence. Machine learning not only requires stats & calculus, it gave her the chance to work on varied and elaborate problems.

Before her first full-time job, she remotely worked on different data analysis internships and freelanced as a Deep Learning Engineer for a Nevada-based company.

Due to the pandemic, she is working remotely, as a Software Engineer at Techment, with gracious and welcoming colleagues. This experience gave her the confidence of working on production-scale projects.

In her free time, she likes to listen to bollywood music and read fictional novels. She loves to travel to different places and enjoys cultural and scenic diversity in India. She hopes to travel abroad and perhaps get an opportunity to travel along with work.
